Set up Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

  1. Start on the service you want to secure. Open its settings and find "Two-Factor Authentication" or similar.
  2. Select "Authenticator app".
  3. Scan the QR code or enter the key. In your authenticator app tap "+" to add a new account.
  4. Enter the 6-digit code. Type the code from the app back into the website.
  5. Save your backup codes.

Popular Authenticator Apps

  • Google Authenticator: Tap the "+" button then "Scan a QR code".
  • Microsoft Authenticator: Sign in if needed, tap "Add account", choose "Other".
  • Authy: Create a free account to sync codes across devices.
  • 1Password: Store your 2FA codes with your passwords.

Examples

Google Account

  1. Visit myaccount.google.com/security.
  2. Under "Signing in to Google" choose "2-Step Verification" > "Authenticator app".
  3. Scan the QR code and enter the 6-digit code.

Facebook

  1. Open Settings > Password and Security.
  2. Under "Two-factor authentication" choose "Authentication App".
  3. Scan the QR code and confirm with the app code.

Instagram

  1. Go to your profile > Menu > Settings and privacy > Accounts Center.
  2. Select Password and security > Two-factor authentication > Authentication app.
  3. Copy or scan the code, then enter the 6-digit code to finish.

Codes refresh every 30 seconds. Transfer your authenticator app before changing phones and keep backup codes safe.
